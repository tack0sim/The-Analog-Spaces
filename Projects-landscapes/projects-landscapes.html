scaping lands

In Spring 2020, just at the beginning of the Corona Virus pandemic, I started out on a new kind of creative journey. I got myself a gravel bike. And in early 2023, I rediscovered my passion for film photography. This blog a documentation of the intersection of these two mediums.

I want to share here some of my impressions since then. It is my hope not to make this a very thic documentation of my creative process, but it should have an understandable structure and logic.

Therefore, this website has a few sections that interlap each other, while others are independant. Some will be follow a narrative style; others will be more perseptive and/or interpretative.

street spirituality

I'm not one for validation of religion. But spirituality is something that we all can experience. I guess, I've had my fair share of spirituality in a non-religious sense. Don't worry, this is not a space for religious chatter. What it is about, is a creative expression of what spirituality sometimes results in.

Starting out as a student of philosophical investigations of our consciousness, spirituality fascinated me quite a bit. I've been careful not to let my personal opinions come in the way of my creative process of writing, but shall we say it isn't always easy.
